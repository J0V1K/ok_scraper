    Scam Alert - Pay Tolls Text Messages - Please be aware there are text messages circulating regarding Toll violations with a fraudulent payment link. Toll fees in Oklahoma are distributed by mail or to a registered Pikepass account. The Supreme Court does not handle cases involving Toll violations or collect fees and penalties. To verify the text messages are legitimate, check with the Oklahoma Turnpike Authority by calling 1-800-745-3727. Do not click links or use phone numbers listed in the scam text messages.
